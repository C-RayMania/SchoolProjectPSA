Pick My Ride is your AI-powered car comparison tool. With us, you can easily compare different car models side-by-side to find the perfect one for your needs.

Start Comparing Cars Now!

How it works

  1. Enter Your Choices: Start clicking on the button above. Then enter the two car models you're considering in the input boxes.
  2. Data Retrieval: Pick My Ride taps into a comprehensive database of car information, sourced from a regularly updated GitHub repository. This ensures you always have access to the latest specs and details.
  3. AI-Powered Insights: Our intelligent algorithm instantly retrieves and compares key features of both cars, including:
    • Make and model
    • Price
    • Engine capacity
    • And more (coming soon!)
  4. Side-by-Side Comparison: The results are presented in a clear and concise table, allowing you to easily visualize the differences and similarities between the two options.
  5. Visual Clues: We highlight the stand-out features with color-coded cells, so you can quickly spot where each car excels. Green indicates a lower price or higher engine capacity, while red signifies the opposite.
  6. Informed Decision: With Pick My Ride's guidance, you can confidently choose the car that best aligns with your needs, budget, and preferences. Say goodbye to endless research and confusing spreadsheets!

Technologies Used

  • HTML, CSS, and JavaScript: These core web development technologies form the backbone of our user-friendly interface and seamless experience.
  • GitHub: We leverage GitHub to host our car database, ensuring data integrity, version control, and easy updates.
  • JSON: This lightweight data format efficiently stores and transmits car information, making it quick and easy for our algorithm to process.
  • jQuery: This popular JavaScript library simplifies interactions with the website and streamlines data retrieval.
